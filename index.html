Let me introduce myself -

My name is Wendy de Lange and I am an upcoming UX UI and front-end designer. I'm already doing this for a couple of years with joy and therefor I'm looking for a new challenge!

I was born in the Netherlands, but I'm half Colombian. Thanks to this I grew op in an environment with multicultural values and languages. I can speak English, Dutch and Spanish fluently. Currently I'm studying at the Hague University for the bachelor Communication & Graphic design.

In my free time I do allot of reading and cycling around the town. Obviously I like the occasional Netflix time somewhere in the week, but hanging out with friends is what I would prefer. Drawing and illustrating is also one of my favorite activities, it's a form of expressing yourself for who you truly are. And I love that!
